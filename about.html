About The Designer

My name is Corbyn. I am a 15 year old male high school junior in CT and I also take early college classes. I suffer from ADHD and SPD, and dissociative amnesia. I enjoy playing volleyball, painting, crocheting, diamond painting, listening to music, sparring, etc. My favorite video game is 'Grounded.'

My definition of "games"

My definition of a game is "an online or offline activity to relieve stress." I personally believe that most, or all games, should at the very least be stress relieving.

My gamer "type(s)" by the Bartle Test"

According to the Bartle Test, I am:

  1. 87% Explorer
  2. 53% Achiever
  3. 47% Killer
  4. 13% Socialiser
I interpret the aforementioned data slightly different than as it appears, as when this test was taken, I solely thought of my favorite game. In this game, to kill is to achieve, and to explore is to kill. For example, in Grounded, to obtain a tier II axe I'd need to kill stinkbugs, meaning I need to explore the haze biome of the map and kill whatever poses as a threat to my goal. While I enjoy playing with other people, I often find myself in a position where I am the only person willing to play. My desires and goals for the game vary when I play with other people.
