Designer Mission

The mission I aim to achieve with my game is to challenge people who, like myself, are on the spectrum. I want to create something that will create great struggle if you don't take it step by step, something like Grounded, where you cannot take shortcuts. In addition I want this game to be fun and push the player to generate their own creative ideas in this sandbox-type game.
